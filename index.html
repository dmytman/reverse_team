How to play

×

The player is offered a field with a number of objects of different design, color and shape. The task is simple - to find two identical objects. Sounds too easy? The game continues until the time expires or until the end of lives, which are lost as a result of incorrect answers. After the game, carefully evaluate your results by comparing them with previous games. At any time, you can analyze the dynamics of your personality development from your personal account, including after completing trainings in the “Find a Pair” program.
